Standards

Methodology

Independent, nonpartisan tracking built on public records — not press releases alone.

What we publish

Map points, meetings and headlines are drawn from agendas, permits, utility filings, official statements and credible reporting. We label each item by confidence level.

Confirmed

A direct public source — government notice, filed permit, signed order or on-the-record statement from an accountable official.

Reported

Credible journalism or trade reporting with named sources. We link to the original article and update if official records contradict it.

Public signal

Social posts or community statements that document the debate. Summaries are written by the tracker; we link to the original post when available.

What we exclude

Anonymous tips without documents, recycled press releases with no new filing, and speculative project rumors with no locatable public record.

Corrections

Errors are corrected when new public records appear. Email michigandatacentertracker@gmail.com with a primary source link.
