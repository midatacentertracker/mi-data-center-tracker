Privacy notice

Your inbox is not a commodity.

This notice explains what happens when you subscribe to the Michigan Data Center Briefing or contact the tracker.

Information we collect

If you subscribe, we collect the email address you provide. If you email a tip, correction or sponsorship inquiry, we receive the information included in your message.

How it is used

Email addresses are used to send the requested briefing, service messages and related tracker updates. Contact messages are used to respond to the purpose for which they were sent.

Mailchimp

The briefing is expected to use Mailchimp to process subscriptions and deliver email. Mailchimp may process subscriber information under its own privacy terms. The final signup form should point only to the tracker’s intended Mailchimp audience.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell subscriber addresses. Information may be shared with service providers needed to operate the briefing. You may unsubscribe through any briefing email or request removal by contacting us.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: michigandatacentertracker@gmail.com.
